Welcome to Grupo Unção Capoeira located in Seattle, Washington!

Our school offers classes in Capoeira: a Brazilian art form which emphasizes strength, balance, coordination, and self-defense. Our training also includes acrobatics, music, traditional dance and Portuguese language. All are welcome!
